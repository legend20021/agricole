Addictive Strawberries and Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash and slice the strawberries.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  3. Layer the strawberries and whipped cream in a serving dish.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.
